About

Strategy from the front lines.
Execution from the ground up.

I started my career in high-stakes financial advisory, where I developed the analytical discipline and decision-making instincts that still shape how I approach every engagement. That foundation gave me something most technology consultants don't have: an understanding of how executives think, how capital flows, and what "good enough" actually costs.

From there I moved into management consulting, eventually joining Publicis Sapient as a Senior Consultant in Data & AI Strategy. Over five years I've led enterprise-scale AI and digital transformation engagements across financial services, retail, and healthcare — working with organizations like Walmart, Visa, and Bank of America to turn AI strategy into measurable operational outcomes. My work spans the full arc: from C-suite roadmaps and competitive assessments to hands-on implementation of agentic AI systems, data infrastructure, and intelligent automation.

I'm trained in both business strategy and modern technical execution — Python, SQL, cloud platforms, and the full agentic AI stack — which means I can sit in a strategy session in the morning and ship working code by afternoon.

Today I work with a select number of clients independently because I believe founders, operators, and mid-market leaders deserve the same depth of strategic and technical rigor that Fortune 500 companies pay for — delivered directly, without layers, and without the overhead.
