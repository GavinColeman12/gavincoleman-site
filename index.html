About

Strategy from the boardroom.
Execution from the engine room.

My career started in mergers & acquisitions, where I advised on complex transactions and learned how great operators make decisions under pressure. That foundation shaped everything about how I work: rigorous analysis, clear priorities, and a bias toward outcomes over output.

Over the last five years I've led technology and transformation projects across industries — building AI-powered tools, automating workflows, and architecting the systems that let teams scale. I've worked with early-stage founders, private equity portfolio companies, and mid-market operators who needed more than slide decks.

Today I run an independent practice because I believe small and medium-sized businesses deserve the same caliber of strategic and technical thinking that Fortune 500 companies pay millions for — delivered personally, practically, and without the overhead.
