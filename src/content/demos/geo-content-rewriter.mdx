## The problem it solves

Search is shifting from "10 blue links" to AI answers that cite specific sources. Most small-business websites were written for 2015-era SEO and are structurally invisible to AI search engines — they lack direct-answer structure, FAQ schema, entity specificity, and citeable claims. This tool audits a website, rewrites the pages that matter, and re-publishes them in a format AI engines can parse and cite.

## Who it's for

Any business that wants to show up in ChatGPT, Perplexity, Google AI Overviews, and Claude when prospects ask generative-search questions. Especially effective for service businesses with a defined service area and a service menu (dental, legal, medspa, home services, local professional services).

## What it does under the hood

The system crawls the target site, scores each page on AI-readability (direct-answer structure, entity mentions, schema coverage, citation density, factual specificity), produces a prioritized rewrite list, and generates the rewrites with human review. JSON-LD schema (FAQPage, LocalBusiness, Service) is injected automatically. Rewrites land as a pull request against the site's repository, or as Webflow / WordPress updates.

## Engagement structure

One week per 20-page section. Week structure — audit day one, rewrite draft days two through four, review and publish day five. Most practices see AI-citation lift within two to six weeks of re-indexing.
