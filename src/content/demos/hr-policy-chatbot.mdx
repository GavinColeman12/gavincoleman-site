## The problem it solves

At every company with more than 15 employees, HR spends a meaningful fraction of each week answering the same dozen questions — PTO policy, holiday schedule, remote work rules, benefits eligibility, expense reimbursement, how to request time off. This chatbot ingests the company handbook and HR policies, then answers any of those questions in natural language, 24/7, with a citation to the specific clause in the source document.

## Who it's for

Businesses with 15–500 employees where HR bandwidth is a real constraint. Especially strong fit for distributed teams (remote workers hitting a question at 9 p.m. their time), multi-location operations, and seasonal businesses where onboarding volume spikes.

## What it does under the hood

Claude-powered retrieval over the company's HR documents. Every answer includes a citation — the specific section of the handbook, PTO policy, benefits guide. The system respects confidentiality (no training on company data, no answer shared outside the authenticated session) and logs every question asked. That log becomes the single most useful HR document in the company: a ranked list of what employees actually want to know.

## Engagement structure

Two weeks from kickoff to deploy. Week one — ingest all handbook and policy documents, define the tone and the escalation rules. Week two — QA the answers across the top 30 anticipated questions, deploy to Slack / Teams / the internal portal. Ongoing cost $200–$500 per month.
